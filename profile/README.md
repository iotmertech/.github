# IOTMER – IoT Platform for Industrial Hardware Manufacturers

**IOTMER** is a secure IoT gateway and platform purpose-built for industrial hardware manufacturers and OEMs. We help companies **connect, manage, and scale** connected devices in industrial and regulated environments — without building infrastructure from scratch.

---

## 🚀 What We Do

We act as the secure bridge between thousands (or millions) of IoT devices and your core enterprise systems — enabling you to deploy connected products quickly, securely, and at scale.

### Key Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Device Connectivity** | MQTT, HTTP, and TCP support for seamless hardware and enterprise integration |
| **Device Management** | Centralized onboarding, authentication, monitoring, and remote management |
| **Data Validation & Security** | Certificate-based identity and policy-driven validation |
| **Enterprise Integration** | Connect IoT data to ERP, billing, analytics, and more |
| **Audit & Deterministic Data** | Full audit trails and replay for regulatory and financial environments |
